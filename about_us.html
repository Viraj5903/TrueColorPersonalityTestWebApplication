About Us

The True Colors Personality Test is a psychological tool designed to identify and categorize individuals into four distinct personality types or "colors": Orange, Green, Blue, and Gold. Each color represents a cluster of personality traits and preferences. The test is often used in educational settings, such as schools, to help individuals gain insight into their own behaviors and better understand the diversity of personalities within a group.
